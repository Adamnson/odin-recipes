Cheese Maggie

Who doesn't love Maggie? Especially with winters just round the corner, the "2-minute-noodles" are just the thing for the cold! From school going kids to teenagers in colleges and working professionals, everyone loves maggie for its tase. And cheese maggie! That's something sacrilegious and the most lustful maggie at the same time. In this article, let's explore some variants of this masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Maggie Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Green Peas
  • Carrots
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take 2 table spoons of oil in an utensil.
  2. Saute some onions. You are aiming for a golden brown color.
  3. You can add the green peas and carrots at this stage.
  4. Add some garam masala and a pinch of salt.
  5. Once the vegetables are cooked, you can add the maggie noodles.
  6. You will have to add a cup of water to cook the maggie noodles.
  7. Add the Maggie Masala that comes with the noodels and let the mixures simmer.
  8. Add another half cup of water to cook the noodles. Be mindful of the amount of water you add. You don't want the maggie to sit in a pool of masala water.
  9. Plate the cooked maggie. Add a slice of cheese on top and savour the sight of the cheese taking on the contour of the maggie.
  10. Grab a fork, dig in!
